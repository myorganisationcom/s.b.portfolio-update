# Popup Diagnosis Questions & Answers

1. **When you look at last month's revenue — what's your honest feeling?**
   - 📉 It dropped or was really inconsistent
   - 😐 Flat — same numbers as always
   - 📈 Growing, but much slower than I want
   - 🔥 Growing fast but I feel out of control

2. **Where do most of your new clients or customers come from today?**
   - 🤝 Referrals & word of mouth only
   - 📱 Social media — but results are inconsistent
   - 🔄 Mostly repeat customers, rarely new ones
   - 🤷 Honestly, I'm not sure

3. **How many hours a week do YOU personally work inside the business?**
   - ⚡ 60+ hours — I'm doing everything myself
   - ⏱️ 40-60 hours — busy but managing
   - 📋 30-40 hours — somewhat delegated
   - 🧘 Under 30 hours — team handles most things

4. **What happens when you try to close a sale or pitch your service?**
   - 😬 Most people say 'too expensive' or ghost me
   - 🔄 They show interest but rarely convert
   - 🙄 I don't have a clear pitch or process
   - ✅ Sales is fine — my other problems are bigger

5. **If I asked about your business systems — what's closest to reality?**
   - 📁 What systems? Everything is in my head
   - 📊 Some spreadsheets and WhatsApp groups
   - 🛠️ We have tools but they're not connected
   - ⚙️ Solid systems — but need to scale them

6. **How clear is your brand positioning — what makes you different?**
   - ❓ We're not very different from competitors
   - 💭 I know it internally but can't explain it clearly
   - 📝 We have a message but it's not converting
   - 🎯 Clear and strong — brand is not my issue

7. **What's the #1 thing you most want to fix in your business right now?**
   - 💰 Get more clients and increase revenue
   - 🏗️ Build proper systems so I can scale
   - 🗺️ Get a clear strategy and direction
   - 🌟 Build a brand people actually know and trust
